  • setupFutureUsage

    Indicates that you intend to make future payments with this PaymentIntents payment method.

    Providing this parameter will attach the payment method to the PaymentIntents Customer, after the PaymentIntent is confirmed and any required actions from the user are complete. If no Customer was provided, the payment method can still be attached to a Customer after the transaction completes.

    Possible values: “on_session”: Use “on_session” if you intend to only reuse the payment method when your customer is present in your checkout flow. “off_session”: Use “off_session” if your customer may or may not be present in your checkout flow.
